Encounters in Econometric Theory

May 14-15, 2026 - Nuffield College, University of Oxford

Motivation

The "Encounters in Econometric Theory" workshop aims to unite young econometricians across Europe, fostering a collaborative environment to discuss and share their research.

Following the success of our previous editions at Nuffield College, Oxford and Paris School of Economics, this year we are very exited come back to Nuffield College, Oxford!

Our goal is to create an intimate space where we can engage with one another, exploring theoretical econometrics and the challenges that inspire us.

This year you will also have the great pleasure and honour of listening to our threee keynote speakers:

Aureo de Paula (University College London)

Maximilian Kasy (University of Oxford)

Daniel Wilhelm (LMU Munich)!
